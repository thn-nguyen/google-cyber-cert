## Document an Incident With an Incident Handler’s Journal

### Overview
This activity used an incident handler’s journal to document and analyze a cybersecurity incident. It focuses on recording key details such as how the incident occurred, its impact, and response actions to build incident documentation skills.

---

### Scenario
A small U.S. healthcare clinic experienced a ransomware attack after attackers used phishing emails to gain access to the network. The malicious attachments inside emails encrypted critical patient data after employees downloaded them into their computer. It is preventing users from accessing systems and causing business operations to shut down. The attackers demanded money in exchange for a decryption key.

---

### 5 W’s of the Incident
- Who: An organized group of unethical hackers.
- What: A ransomware security incident.
- When: Tuesday at 9:00 a.m.
- Where: A small U.S. healthcare clinic.
- Why: Attackers gained access through phishing emails containing malicious attachments. After installation of the malware, ransomware was deployed to encrypt files. The motivation was likely financial gain.

---

### Impact
- Prevented employees from accessing patient records. 
- Disrupted normal business operations and forced the healthcare clinic to shut down its computer systems. 
- Caused operational downtime.

---

### Response
The clinic shut down its computer systems and reported the incident to relevant authorities for technical assistance.

---

### Recommendations
- Establish an IT department.
- Employee training.
- Apply NIST CSF framework.

---

### What I Learned?
Importance of cybersecurity awareness, security controls, and incident response plan to ensure security posture in the organizations.
The attacker's methods and risks in phishing attacks, which is an entry point for ransomware.
